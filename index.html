Championship Stories

1999–00
Galatasaray
The Year Europe Stood Still
Under Fatih Terim, Galatasaray achieved something no Turkish club had done before or since. They won the UEFA Cup in a penalty shootout against Arsenal, then beat Real Madrid to claim the UEFA Super Cup — all while capturing the Süper Lig title. Hakan Şükür, Hasan Şaş and a golden generation created an era that defined a club and a nation.
UEFA Cup Super Cup Süper Lig Fatih Terim
2004–07
Fenerbahçe
The Alex Epoch
When Alex de Souza stepped onto Turkish soil, Fenerbahçe found their heartbeat. Three league titles in four seasons (2004, 2005, 2007) cemented his legacy as the greatest import in Turkish football history. His free-kicks became folklore, his performances in Istanbul derbies became myth. Opponents feared him. Fenerbahçe loved him.
3× Şampiyon Alex de Souza Derby Dominance
2010–11
Fenerbahçe
Kocaman's Unbeaten Run
Aykut Kocaman's Fenerbahçe went on a legendary unbeaten run through 2010–11 that seemed unstoppable. With Selçuk Şahin orchestrating from midfield and Edu, Cristian and Deivid providing firepower, they stormed to the title with style. A 2-1 win over Galatasaray in May 2011 was a declaration of dominance.
